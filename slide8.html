Future Skills Development

Preparing for the AI-Driven Economy

The changing job market will demand a hybrid skillset:

Technical Proficiencies:

  • AI literacy and understanding
  • Data analysis capabilities
  • Digital collaboration tools
  • Computational thinking

Human Skills:

  • Critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Adaptability and resilience
  • Emotional intelligence
  • Effective collaboration

As AI takes over routine tasks, uniquely human capabilities become increasingly valuable. By 2030, employers anticipate a significant shift in core skills needed, with technological literacy and human-centric skills becoming paramount.
