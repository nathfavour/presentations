Challenges and Concerns

Navigating the Complexities of AI in Education

Ethical Considerations:

  • Algorithmic bias perpetuating inequities
  • Privacy and security of student data
  • Academic integrity with AI-generated content

Digital Divide:

  • Unequal access to technology
  • Disparities in internet connectivity
  • Potential to widen educational gaps

Implementation Hurdles:

  • Inadequate teacher training and support
  • Low AI literacy among educators
  • Risk of over-reliance reducing critical thinking
  • Diminished human interaction
  • Cybersecurity vulnerabilities

The successful integration of AI in education requires addressing these challenges with carefully considered policies and practices.
