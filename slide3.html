AI in K-12 Education

Transforming Early Learning Experiences

AI is fundamentally changing how younger students learn, providing personalized experiences while supporting teachers in new ways.

Applications:

  • Personalized learning pathways
  • Automated administrative tasks
  • Supplementing educational content
  • Making complex concepts accessible
  • Supporting diverse learning needs

Key Concerns:

  • Academic integrity (cheating, plagiarism)
  • Insufficient teacher training
  • Data privacy and security
  • Algorithmic bias
  • Reduced human interaction
